The Best BLT

The best BLT is an acronym for Bacon, Lettuce, and Tomato. Add all this ingrediants and put them between two peices of bread and you got yourself a BLT sandwhich.

  • bread
  • mayonaise
  • tomato
  • Bacon
  1. cook Bacon until slightly crispy
  2. toast bread to desired crispiness
  3. cut tomatoes
  4. break down head of lettuce
