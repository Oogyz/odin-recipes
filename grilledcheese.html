Grilled Cheese

Two slices a bread with cheese between grilled on a stove. Often made with butter on one side so they don't stick to the pan. butter is sometimes replaced with mayonaise.

  • bread
  • butter
  • cheese
  1. butter pan
  2. wait until butter is melted
  3. lay one piece of bread on pan then cheese and then bread on top of the cheese
  4. cook until bread is gold or desired color
